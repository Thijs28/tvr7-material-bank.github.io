1. Pre-Demolition Mass
(Oorspronkelijk Volume)

The TVR7 complex is redefined as a temporary ledger of physical assets. Prior to intervention, the site encompasses a dense structural volume including 1,098 m³ of embedded earthworks (grondwerken), representing a massive consolidation of raw material waiting for phase-shifted extraction.

2. Selective Deconstruction
(Selectieve Ontmanteling)

Profitability is achieved through hyper-specific dismantling prior to heavy demolition. High-yield assets are isolated: 32.21 m³ of brickwork (baksteen) and 145.96 m² of blue stone flooring (blauwe steen) are extracted completely intact, entering the secondary market to offset overarching costs.

3. The Permanent Skeleton
(Permanent Skelet)

Following the harvest of reusable materials, the subtractive sequence concludes. Over 1,160 m² of concrete slabs (betonvloeren) and 899 m² of exterior joinery (buitenschrijnwerk) are permanently removed. The remaining artifact is a stripped, load-bearing framework.
